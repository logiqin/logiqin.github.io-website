AI-Boosted Admin: Automate. Accelerate. Improve.

By Franck Clowez • September 15, 2025

"From repetitive tasks to intelligent assistance—AI that makes administration effortless."

The Problem with Traditional Administrative Workflows

Administrative staff, assistants, and managers often struggle with:

  • • Manual copy-paste across systems and documents
  • • Time-consuming email drafting and responses
  • • Slow creation of reports and presentations

This leads to wasted time, inconsistent quality, and missed opportunities for automation.

Value Delivered

  • • 10–30% time savings on daily administrative tasks
  • • Faster, higher-quality communication in email, reports, and meeting minutes
  • • Hands-on adoption of AI tools — MS Copilot (inside/outside MS 365), Copilot Studio, Gamma (for presentations), plus GPT, Mistral, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini
  • • Team enablement through best-practice exchange, supporting all levels from casual users to power users who can build and share AI agents

How It Works: Three Steps

1. Preparation & Assessment

  • • Assemble a group of up to 10 people with similar roles
  • • Gather examples of current workflows and map what's been tried with AI (successes and failures)

2. Interactive Training Workshop

Live demonstrations of high-impact AI use cases:

  • • Email handling, meeting minutes, slide creation, personal RAG assistants
  • • Address participants' specific questions with tailored guidance
  • • Hands-on exercises to automate real everyday processes

3. Consolidation & Team Sharing

  • • Guide participants in building and sharing simple AI agents
  • • Define quality controls to ensure accuracy and compliance
  • • Document best practices for reuse in future projects and teams
